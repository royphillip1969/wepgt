About WEP Global Tech

WEP Global Tech is a technology consulting firm specializing in cloud infrastructure, broadcast systems engineering, and IT operations. Founded by Elroy Phillip, a seasoned Cloud Infrastructure Consultant and Broadcast Systems Administrator, our mission is to help organizations modernize, secure, and optimize their technology environments.

With a foundation built on decades of experience in broadcast engineering and cloud architecture, WEP Global Tech bridges the gap between traditional media infrastructure and next-generation cloud ecosystems. Our expertise spans AWS cloud solutions, systems automation, network design, hybrid integrations, and operational reliability.

Whether supporting control room systems for major broadcast networks or deploying scalable AWS cloud platforms for enterprise clients, WEP Global Tech delivers technology solutions that are secure, reliable, and performance-driven.

In addition to client services, we develop innovative internal tools such as the R.A.T. (Report & Transmit) platform for security reporting and the Resident-Verifier application for property management — reflecting our commitment to practical innovation and operational excellence.

Projects & Case Studies

R.A.T. – Report & Transmit Platform

Developed a secure web-based security incident reporting system with automated notifications, audit-ready logs, and role-based access control. Built with Node.js, Express, Prisma, and SQLite, this internal platform improves situational awareness and compliance tracking for on-site teams.

Resident-Verifier Application

Engineered a data-import tool for property management systems that verifies resident records and payment details directly from PDF rent rolls. Built using Node.js and SQLite, it streamlines data accuracy, eliminates manual errors, and supports secure verification workflows.

Expense Tracker Platform

Designed a lightweight financial management and expense tracking web app for personal and small business use. Incorporates real-time summaries, local database persistence, and responsive UI built with Express.js and EJS templates for modern usability.
